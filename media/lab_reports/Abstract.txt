Abstract
This project presents a Hospital Management System (HMS) designed to modernize healthcare delivery by replacing error-prone paper records with a unified digital platform. The system addresses two critical challenges: illegible handwriting and fragmented communication between departments such as laboratories, pharmacies, and wards, both of which contribute to patient delays and inefficiencies.
The HMS integrates an HTML/CSS/JavaScript frontend with a robust Django (Python) backend, ensuring centralized access to patient records, secure prescriptions, and digitalized laboratory reports. By enabling real-time data sharing across departments, the system fosters coordinated care and minimizes administrative bottlenecks.
The primary objective is to reduce patient queues and waiting times by eliminating paperwork errors and streamlining hospital workflows, thereby saving valuable time for both patients and healthcare staff.
The system is structured around six core modules:
- Patient Management – Registration and maintenance of Electronic Health Records (EHR).
- Appointment & Scheduling – Efficient booking, tracking, and optimization of patient visits.
- Doctor/Physician Module – Access to EHRs, digital prescription issuance, and diagnosis recording.
- Laboratory Management – Digital test requests, sample tracking, and instant report publication.
- Pharmacy & Inventory Management – Processing prescriptions and monitoring drug stock levels.
- Billing & Administration – Automated invoice generation, payment tracking, and financial reporting.
Together, these modules form a comprehensive solution that enhances accuracy, efficiency, and patient satisfaction in hospital operations.
