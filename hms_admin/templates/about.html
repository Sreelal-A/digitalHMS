System Overview

This system replaces traditional paper-based hospital management with a more organized and efficient digital solution. Staff can manage patient details, appointments, prescriptions, laboratory reports, medicines, and bills all from one place.

The system improves communication between departments and reduces common issues such as record loss, handwriting errors, billing mistakes, and delays in patient service.

Role-based access ensures each user sees only what is relevant to their department, keeping data secure and operations streamlined.
